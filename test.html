Why Sherlock Holmes?

          

            I first discovered Sherlock Holmes through the BBC's Sherlock
            series, and Benedict Cumberbatch's performance made me fall in love
            with the character. For me, Benedict will always be the perfect
            Sherlock! After watching the series, I started reading Sir Arthur
            Conan Doyle's books and became fascinated by Holmes' brilliance and
            unique personality.
          

          

            Sherlock Holmes inspires me because he's clever, determined, and
            always curious. That's why I chose to dedicate this website to him,
            the world's greatest detective.

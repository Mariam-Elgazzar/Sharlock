BBC's Sherlock

Benedict Cumberbatch and Martin Freeman star as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. John Watson in the hit BBC series “Sherlock.” The duo take on their familiar roles in a more modern setting: contemporary London. The show began in 2012, has run for four non-consecutive seasons, and has won several British Academy Television Awards. The show has introduced a whole new generation of fans to the world of Sherlock Holmes, and it has developed a very large fan community, especially on the blogging site tumblr.
